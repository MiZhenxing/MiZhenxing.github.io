Brief Biography
======   
I am currently a Ph.D student in Department of 
[Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.cse.ust.hk/) 
at [HKUST](https://www.ust.hk/home).
My supervisor is Prof. [Dan Xu](https://www.danxurgb.net/). 
I am interested in recovering 3D structures from videos and multi-views by deep learning methods. I am currently working on deep visual slam, structure from motion and multi-view stereo.
