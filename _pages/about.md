## Brief Biography

I am currently a Ph.D student in [Department of Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.cse.ust.hk/) 
at [HKUST](https://www.ust.hk/home).
My supervisor is Prof. [Dan Xu](https://www.danxurgb.net/). 
I am interested in recovering 3D structures from videos and multiple images by deep learning methods. I am currently working on deep visual SLAM, structure from motion and multi-view stereo. I also pay attention to the latest developments of other 3D researches such as point cloud parsing, 
surface reconstruction, etc.
