Zhenxing Mi

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Prof. Dan Xu's Vision Group, at the HKUST.

I received my Bachelor's Degree and Master's Degree in Automation from AIA at HUST in China, advised by Prof. Wenbing Tao. I was an intern at Snap Research in 2024, working with Kuan-Chieh Wang, Guocheng Qian and Kfir Aberman.

I have broad research interests on 3D reconstruction such as scalable NeRF with Mixture of Experts (MoE), Multi-view Stereo and 3D surface reconstruction. I am currently interested in multimodal generation, vision-language models and 3D world models. I mainly research on scalable NeRF, multimodal generation and 3D world models currently.
