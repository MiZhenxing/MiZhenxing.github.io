Zhenxing Mi

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Prof. Dan Xu's Vision Group, at the HKUST.

I received my Bachelor's Degree and Master's Degree in Automation from AIA at HUST in China, advised by Prof. Wenbing Tao. I was an intern at Snap Research in 2024, working with Kuan-Chieh Wang, Guocheng Qian and Kfir Aberman.

I have broad research interests in multimodal understanding and generation, and 3D / 4D reconstruction and generation. I am currently working on multimodal generation and 4D world models.
