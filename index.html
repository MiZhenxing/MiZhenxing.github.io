Zhenxing Mi 密振兴

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Prof. Dan Xu's Vision Group, at the HKUST.

I received my Bachelor's Degree and Master's Degree in Automation from AIA at HUST in China, advised by Prof. Wenbing Tao.

I am interested in recovering 3D structures from videos and multiple images by deep learning methods. I am currently working on Nerf and Multi-view Stereo. I also pay attention to the latest developments of other 3D researches such as deep visual SLAM, structure from motion, point cloud parsing, surface reconstruction, etc.
